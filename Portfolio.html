Chart

Having attended the Economics Observatory Lectures, Oxford Professor Abi Adams-Prassl mentioned the UK had record high employment in March 2020. Advanced Analytics

Chart

This chart is an excerpt from my project. It studies the correlation between Freedom and GDP per Capita and uses to Loess regression to create a line of best fit. The benefit of this over the linear regression is it can change across the range, however when there is little data, outliers can drastically effect the average. The size of the circles are reflective of that nation's population.
